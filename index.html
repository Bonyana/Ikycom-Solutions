Who we are

IkyCom Solutions is a Botswana-based Information and Communications Technology (ICT) consultancy specialising in IT Service Management (ITSM), digital transformation, and enterprise service management. We help organisations improve service delivery, governance and operational efficiency through pragmatic technology frameworks and strategic advisory.

Since founding, IkyCom has delivered complex ITSM and knowledge-management implementations for major enterprises — including AbariCom Botswana. Our work there established ITSM frameworks, governance structures and knowledge databases that enabled the delivery of NOC-as-a-Service to the financial sector.

Our approach integrates people, process and technology to deliver measurable business impact. We specialise in IT governance, process automation, change management and enterprise digital strategy — helping organisations achieve sustainable digital maturity.
