Meet Our Founder

Vision. Integrity. Innovation. The principles that define IkyCom Solutions.

Ikanyeng Molemele

Ikanyeng Molemele is a UK- and Botswana-based ICT consultant specialising in IT Service Management (ITSM), Enterprise Service Management, and IT Governance. Through IkyCom Solutions, she has successfully implemented key IT initiatives for Abaricom, including ITSM frameworks, knowledge management databases, service components, and comprehensive monitoring strategies.

Since 2018, she has served as a strategic advisor to Dr. Wallace Ogufere and Eclat Healthcare, supporting the UK- and Nigeria-based health tech company through its acquisition by Interswitch and expansion into Southern Africa.

Ikanyeng’s career includes leading major projects for global organizations such as Vodafone, Huawei, the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development, Atos Origin (London Olympics 2012), and Capgemini.

She is passionate about mentorship, innovation, and empowering young professionals to thrive in digital transformation and governance roles.
