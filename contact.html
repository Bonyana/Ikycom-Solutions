About IkyCom Solutions

Empowering organizations through Digital Innovation, ITSM, and Enterprise Service Management Solutions.

IkyCom Solutions is a Botswana-based Information and Communications Technology (ICT) company with deep expertise in IT Service Management (ITSM), digital transformation, and enterprise service management. The company has built a strong reputation for helping organizations enhance service delivery, governance, and operational efficiency through innovative technology frameworks and strategic consulting.

IkyCom has successfully delivered complex ITSM and knowledge management implementations for major enterprises — including AbariCom Botswana — where it led the establishment of ITSM frameworks, governance structures, and knowledge management databases that enabled the company to offer Network Operations Centre (NOC) as a Service to the financial sector.

IkyCom’s approach integrates people, processes, and technology to deliver measurable business impact. Its strategic expertise covers IT governance, process automation, change management, and enterprise digital strategy — empowering organizations to achieve sustainable digital maturity.

Risk management is at the heart of IkyCom’s consulting philosophy — helping clients eliminate operational risk, maintain trust, and enhance technology-driven brand performance.
